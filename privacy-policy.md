# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

## YouTube AI Chatbot Extension

This Privacy Policy explains how the YouTube AI Chatbot Chrome Extension uses information.

## Information We Process

The extension processes:

- The URL of the currently open YouTube video.
- The transcript of the YouTube video.
- Questions entered by the user.

This information is used solely to provide AI-powered answers and summaries.

## Data Storage

The extension may store user preferences locally using Chrome Storage.

## Data Sharing

The extension sends the YouTube video URL and the user's question to a secure backend API to generate AI responses.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect transmitted data.
